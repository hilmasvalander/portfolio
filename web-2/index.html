The App

Statera optimizes your performance through data about recovery, nutrition and training. It calculates your everyday balance between these factors and therefore help you make changes in order to optimize your lifestyle so you can perform even better!

Features

User friendly

Fast, simple, user friendly and 100% mobile

Track your training

Store data about completed training sessions and their intensity

Track your balance

Effectively show the connections between the stored data in order to optimise performance

See risk of injuries

Actively store and calculate "acute-to-chronic-workload-ratio" to warn you when you're in a zone of increased risk for injuries

A tool for everyone:

To help individuals, teams and organisations gain a better balance in their life, Statera is created to suit all needs. Are you an athlete, coach, PT, or looking to improve the life style of your organisation? Click here to find out what is best for you!
